The Open Source Search Engine

Whitespider is a search engine written in PHP. It is designed to be fast, lightweight, and easy to use.

Features

Open Source

Whitespider is released under the MIT license, which means you can use, modify, and distribute it freely.

Multilingual

Whitespider supports multiple languages and can be translated into any language you want.

Community

Whitespider has an active community of developers and users who contribute to its development and support.
